## North Vancouver RapidBuses

### Lonsdale - Lynn Valley

This RapidBus aims to connect the busiest parts of Lonsdale Avenue and Lynn Valley Road. 

### North Shore Orbital

This RapidBus serves as an orbital line for the North Shore, taking advantage of the Trans Canada Highway for fast speeds. It orbits Lonsdale Quay, connecting Phibbs exchange \(which will host an [Inlet line](inlet) station\), the above Lonsdale - Lynn Valley line, and Lions Gate Village \(also the host of an Inlet line station\), mostly on or near the highway.

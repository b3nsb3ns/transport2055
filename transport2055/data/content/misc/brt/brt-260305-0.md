## Bus Rapid Transit

Given this plan's "bold but restrained" approach, and upgrades that I envision for [RapidBus discussed in its relevant page](rapidbus), it does not include BRT beyond what's currently planned by TransLink due to a prediction that many corridors should jump to SkyTrain, and my belief that RapidBus should receive many of the qualities that has, with RapidBus and BRT services folded together into a coherent higher-order urban bus layer. 

### King George Boulevard

With the KGB SkyTrain in the plan, the BRT on this corridor is truncated, terminating at Newton Exchange. 

### Langley - Haney Place

This BRT line is unchanged from the current plans, as I do not anticipate it being over capacity.

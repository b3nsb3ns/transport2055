## Bus

Buses are the workhorses of the transit network, and are likely to continue to carry the majority of ridership. TransLink has excellent plans to increase and improve service, such as more frequency and express versions of certain routes. I also expect more bus lanes and signal priority \([read more on this here](rapidbus)\). Given the countless number of local buses, I can only give a few suggestions. 

### 54th/57th Ave

There should be a bus that runs on 54th/57th Avenue in Vancouver. 

### Canada Way/8th St

An express overlay for the 123 New Westminster - Brentwood should be created, and also adjusted in the context of this plan, including an eastward extension to Columbia Station \(to transfer with the [Expo line's branches](expo) and the [FVX](fvx)\). It should also continue westward on Canada Way past BCIT, as the [Inlet SkyTrain](inlet) now takes its north-south role and there's currently no east-west link between Boundary and Willingdon on Canada Way.

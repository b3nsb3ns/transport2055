# Welcome to Transport 2055

Welcome to Transport 2055, a proposed future for transit in Metro Vancouver. My motivation for creating this is two fold. 

1. Demonstrate my technical abilities. 
2. Combine the various transit plans that exist for the region into one master plan, and give my own thoughts on the matter. 

On the latter, this has turned out to be an integrated transit plan, encompassing many layers, all intertwined into an integrated network that I hope will inspire others in some way. Having a transit network, such as the one proposed here, for the region is one of the determining factors of a prosperous, resilient, and pleasant region. We should strive to never stop improving and building on our transit network, and I would go as far to say this applies to transit across the globe, but since I live in British Columbia, I'm especially thinking about TransLink, BC Transit, BC Ferries, etc.

# Bonus Phases

This section is a collection of ideas that I've had throughout the history of the project that, one way or another, do not fit into the 'main plan'. Initially, it was lines I believed to be necessary within the time window, with the window being between now and 2055. But as this plan grew more 'pessimistic' or 'focused', I've grown to  consider the lines included here to be either obvious next steps or superfluous ideas. Therefore, I will not include these projects from here on out, partly from a desire to focus on the main plan, and partly because I cannot predict the future. This section will instead host additional lines of particular interest or nuance being on their own page. Obvious/superfluous ideas will be included below.  

Skytrain:
- Hastings SkyTrain eastward extension to Kensington Park
- King George Boulevard SkyTrain southern extension
- R6 BRT Skytrain replacement
- Millennium Line Maple Ridge extension

Regional Rail:
- Tsawwassen Express, replacing the [Richmond-Tsawwassen ExpressBus](rt)
- Fraser Valley Express Chilliwack extension

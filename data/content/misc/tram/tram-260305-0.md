## LandTrain (Tram)

This tram is based on several proposed tram projects. I believe it can serve as an orbital route that can connect key destinations, such as Arbutus station, Senakw, Granville Island, the False Creek area including Olympic Village, Chinatown, Gastown, and Coal Harbour. 

### Extension to Marine Drive Station

As part of the next phase of this line, this line is extended south to Marine Drive station, following the Arbutus greenway.

## Bus Rapid Transit

Given this plan's "bold but restrained" approach, it does not include BRT beyond what's currently planned by TransLink due to a prediction that many corridors should jump to SkyTrain, and RapidBus already having many of the qualities that BRT already has. 

### King George Boulevard

With the KGB SkyTrain in the plan, the BRT on this corridor is truncated, terminating at Newton Exchange. 

### Langley - Haney Place

This BRT line is unchanged from the current plans, as I do not anticipate it being over capacity.

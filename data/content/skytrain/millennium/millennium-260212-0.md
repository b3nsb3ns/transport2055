## Millennium Line

The Millennium line will continue to have extensions, both west and east. 

### UBC Extension

The UBC extension is likely the next extension for this line, and will allow TransLink to divert resources now that there is less pressure on buses to serve the university. This extension will also serve the Jericho lands development, and enable connections to and from this future high density and intensity node. 

### Port Coquitlam Extension

This extension aims to make use of the track stubs that exist at Coquitlam Central station. It allows for a seamless extension that minimizes passenger and operation friction, as it can be more-or-less isolated until the extension is complete. This extension also paves the way to another extension to [Maple Ridge](millenniumextend).

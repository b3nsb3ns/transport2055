## King George Boulevard SkyTrain Line

This line is to address capacity concerns for the KGB BRT project. I expect the BRT line built for this corridor to have insufficient capacity, and justify SkyTrain. This sentiment can be considered true today, with the R1 RapidBus being in TransLink's top 10 bus routes by ridership. 

A similar situation can be found with the [R6 RapidBus](r6).

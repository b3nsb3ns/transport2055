## Amtrak Cascades

This is a line that takes advantage of the new [Fraser Valley Express](fvx) tracks, allowing another station for this service to exist in South Surrey. The new tracks for this can also be used to facilitate regional rail, and plug into the FVX mainline. 

Amtrak's recent acquisition of "Airo" trainsets also play well to integration with FVX, as they are equipped with a pantograph to receive power from an electric overhead wire, which FVX should also have.

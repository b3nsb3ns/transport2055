## Sea-to-Sky Express

This is a replacement for coach bus services to the Sea-to-Sky corridor \(that I presume will exist soonish\), allowing for an alternate route that can run in the event of a hihgway closure/obstruction, allowing for transit in this region and to the rest of the Lower Mainland to be better. 

It can also serve as a tourist route, bringing people to and from Metro Vancouver, the beautiful Sea-to-Sky corridor, and the places within \(like Whistler, for example\), giving passengers, local and tourist alike, a vista of this region's natural beauty.

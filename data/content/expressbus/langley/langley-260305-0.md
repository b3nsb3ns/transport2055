## Langley ExpressBuses

These buses allow easy, high quality access to and from Langley. These include ExpressBuses that rapidly connect Langley to Newton \(in Surrey\), Aldergrove/Abbotsford, and White Rock, all of which terminate at the Expo line's Willowbrook station. 

### Langley - Newton

Allows for rapid east-west connections, connecting Newton exchange \(and Newton in general\), CLoverdale, and Langley City. This service also has transfer opportunities to the King George Boulevard SkyTrain and BRT. 

### Langley - Algergrove/Abbotsford

Connects Langley to the western edges of Abbotsford. Allows connections to [Fraser Valley Express](fvx) regional rail in Abbotsford from Aldergrove. 

### Langley - White Rock

Connects Langley to White Rock, and provides a rapid-transit like service to communities on the way.

## Richmond

### Garden City Road/Granville Ave

This line serves as an orbital line that connects the Olympic Oval, Lansdowne, Kwantlen Polytechnic University, Garden City Road, and Bridgeport station. 

### Steveston Highway

This line runs on Steveston Highway, connecting Steveston, the [Canada line](canada), Ironwood Plaza, various ExpressBus lines, and Riverport.

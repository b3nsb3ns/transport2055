## Surrey East-West

These lines are services that aim to tackle a newer demand in Surrey, which is east-west routes.

## 104 Avenue + Midtown (88 Avenue)

This line provides east-west connectivity for Surrey, connecting and feeding into all the rail services in Surrey and the [Surrey Central - Bridgeport ExpressBus](sew). 

## 96 Avenue

This line, like the above provides connectivity to many rail/higher order bus services, and terminates at Carvolth Exchange in Langley Township.
